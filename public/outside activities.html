Staying Active

What do we all need to keep our health up? Exercise! Admittedly this is a struggle for me. I need to keep the balance of healthy body and healthy mind. However, it's a push to want to go on a jog or run. I do enjoy the casual skateboard session here and there, thats easy. Skating is a mood for me though. I get in the mood and go once every month or so. Definitely not enough to keep up a healthy body. So, morning jogs it is! Release those endorphins.
